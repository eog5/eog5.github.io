The Mars Telecommunications Constellation is a proposed mission developed by Erik Gustafson. The driving requirement for the mission is to provide assets on the surface of Mars with continuous high-speed ( >32 mbps) communication relays. The proposed architecture is a constellation of 8 12U cubeSats, each equipped with a solar sail and an optical communications system.
