Mars Telecommunications CubeSat Constellation

The Mars Telecommunications Constellation is a proposed mission being developed by Erik Gustafson. Its goal is to provide assets on the surface of Mars with continuous high-speed ( >32 mbps) communication relays. The proposed architecture is a constellation of 8 12U cubeSats, each equipped with a solar sail and an optical communications system. The following paper is a draft, and is being written for Cornell University's MAE 6530 course. The full version will be completed in May 2019.
